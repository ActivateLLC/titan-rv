Frequently Asked Questions

Sourcing a suitable used mining haul truck (e.g., Caterpillar 793/797, Komatsu 830E/930E) typically ranges from $500,000 to over $3 million, contingent on model, age, operational hours, and condition. We facilitate sourcing or can convert your existing asset.

Operating a TITAN RV generally requires a Commercial Driver's License (CDL) with specific endorsements due to the vehicle's size and weight. Regulations vary by jurisdiction. We offer consultation and can arrange specialized driver training programs.

Timelines vary based on complexity. Foundational conversions typically require 8-12 months. Highly bespoke projects with advanced systems integration can extend to 18-24 months or more from commission to delivery.

While built on platforms designed for extreme durability (50,000+ operational hours), regular preventative maintenance is crucial. We provide detailed service protocols and offer comprehensive global maintenance and support packages starting from $25,000 annually.

Public road access is subject to local regulations regarding oversized vehicles. TITAN RVs excel on private land, designated off-highway routes, and in remote environments. We provide guidance on legal operation and permit acquisition assistance.

Yes, we collaborate with specialized financial institutions experienced in high-value asset financing. Options are available for qualified clients, typically involving tailored terms based on the project scope and client profile.
